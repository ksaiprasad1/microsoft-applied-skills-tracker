## Time Management Tips

- **Daily**: 1-2 hours of focused study
- **Weekly**: Complete 1-2 modules + practice labs
- **Monthly**: Complete 2-3 Applied Skills assessments
- **Breaks**: Take regular breaks to avoid burnout
